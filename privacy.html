Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Vitox ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our platform at vitox.ai and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account — name, email address, profile information
  • Upload content — video files, titles, descriptions, thumbnails
  • Interact with content — comments, likes, subscriptions
  • Use our services — search queries, viewing history, preferences

We also collect information automatically when you use Vitox:

  • Device information — IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, clicks and interactions
  • Cookies and similar technologies — to remember your preferences and provide relevant content

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process account registration and manage your account
  • Personalize your content feed and recommendations
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and promotions
  • Display relevant advertisements from Google AdSense and other partners
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve platform performance
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

Vitox uses Google AdSense to display advertisements on our content pages. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your interests and browsing behavior across the web.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to Vitox and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent — when you choose to share content publicly on the platform
  • Service providers — trusted third parties who assist us in operating our platform (e.g., Cloudinary for video storage)
  • Legal requirements — when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Video content is hosted via Cloudinary, a leading cloud media management platform. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure connections (HTTPS), and access controls to protect your personal information.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out — opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Cookie preferences — manage cookies through your browser settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@vitox.ai.

7. Cookies

Vitox uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the platform to function (e.g., login sessions)
  • Analytics cookies — to understand how users interact with our platform
  • Advertising cookies — used by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads on content pages

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our platform.

8. Children's Privacy

Vitox is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it immediately. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@vitox.ai.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Vitox after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: support@vitox.ai
