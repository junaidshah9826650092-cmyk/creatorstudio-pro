Copyright & Policy

Vitox respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users to do the same. This policy outlines our procedures for addressing copyright infringement claims.

Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe your copyrighted work has been uploaded to Vitox without authorization, please submit a formal DMCA take-down notice. Your notice must include:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  • Identification of the infringing material (URL of the video).
  • Your contact information (Email, Name).
  • A statement of good faith belief that the use is not authorized.

Submit a Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was removed by mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notice through our Creator Studio dashboard or via email.

Repeat Infringer Policy

We terminate the accounts of users who are determined to be repeat infringers in accordance with our terms of service.
