Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a creator on Vitox?
Simply sign up with your Google account and start uploading! Vitox is open to all creators. Once you've uploaded content, you can access your Creator Studio from the dashboard to manage your videos, view analytics, and track your growth.
How does monetization work on Vitox?
Vitox has a built-in points system that rewards creators for engagement and content quality. We also offer brand collaboration features that allow creators to connect with advertisers directly. Monetization eligibility is based on subscriber count, view count, and account standing.
What video formats are supported?
Vitox supports all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and more. Videos are hosted via Cloudinary's global content delivery network for fast, reliable playback worldwide. There is no set file size limit, but very large files may take longer to upload.
How do I report inappropriate content?
If you encounter content that violates our Community Guidelines, you can report it using the report button available on every video page. Our moderation team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action, which may include content removal or account suspension.
How do I delete my account?
To request account deletion, please email us at support@vitox.ai with the subject "Account Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days and delete all associated data per our Privacy Policy.
Is Vitox free to use?
Yes! Vitox is completely free to use for both creators and viewers. We are supported by advertising on content pages. Creators can earn through our monetization programs, and viewers can watch all content for free without any subscription fees.
