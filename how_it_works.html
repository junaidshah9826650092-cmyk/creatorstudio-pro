How Vitox Works

The Next-Gen Indian Video Platform designed for You.

Welcome to Vitox! We believe in giving every creator a voice and every viewer an incredible, personalized experience. Whether you're here to watch or create, this is how we make the magic happen.

Create & Upload

Creating content on Vitox is easy and accessible:

  • Upload: Easily upload high-quality videos or short V-Snaps directly from your computer or phone.
  • AI Studio: We provide built-in AI tools to help you generate eye-catching thumbnails, script ideas, and video enhancements effortlessly.
  • Broadcast Live: Engage deeply with your audience by going live in the Vitox Broadcast Studio.

The Squad & Community

Your followers are not just numbers—they are your Squad. The Squad feed allows you to directly reach your most loyal fans without algorithms getting in the way. Post text updates, polls, and announcements directly to your community.

The Discovery Algorithm

Unlike old platforms that only favor huge creators, the Vitox recommendation engine is designed to give small, talented creators real visibility. When you post a new video, our algorithm tests it with a small audience. If the engagement (watch time, likes, comments) is high, it automatically pushes your video to the "For You" page of thousands more users.

Monetization & Earning

We want you to succeed financialy. You can earn through:

  • AdSense Integration: Once eligible, easily link your Google AdSense account and keep 100% of the revenue generated from your linked ad units.
  • Collab Deals & Sponsorships: Use our integrated Collab Chat to negotiate brand deals directly without leaving the platform.
  • Premium & Tips: Fans can support you through premium features and direct tips (Coming Soon).
