About Vitox

We're building the next generation of video sharing — a platform where creators thrive, audiences connect, and content truly matters.

Our Mission

Vitox was founded with a simple but powerful belief: every creator deserves a fair platform. In a world where video content is the primary medium of expression, we saw an opportunity to build something different — a platform that puts creators first.

We are a video sharing and creator monetization platform designed to give creators the tools, audience, and revenue they need to do what they love full time. Whether you're a gaming streamer, a music artist, a tech educator, or a comedian, Vitox is built for you.

We believe that quality content should be rewarded, that creators should own their audience relationship, and that the internet can be a place of genuine creativity and connection — not just algorithms and ad revenue chasing.

What We Offer

Vitox provides a comprehensive creator ecosystem with features that matter:

Video Publishing

Upload and share long-form videos with HD quality streaming, powerful thumbnails, and SEO-friendly metadata.

V-Snaps (Shorts)

Create and share short-form vertical videos — perfect for quick content, highlights, and trending moments.

Live Streaming

Go live directly from your browser or connect via OBS Studio. Engage your audience in real-time.

Creator Monetization

Earn through our built-in points system, brand collaborations, and soon — direct fan monetization.

Squad Community

Build your squad — a loyal fanbase that follows your content, engages with posts, and supports your growth.

Creator Analytics

Real-time insights into your channel performance, viewer demographics, and content trends.

Our Story

Vitox started as a passion project — a vision for what a video platform could be if it truly centered the creator experience. We were tired of platforms where creators were treated as content supply for an advertising machine, where the algorithm decided who succeeded, and where genuine community building was secondary to engagement metrics.

We launched Vitox to change that. From day one, we've focused on building a platform that is transparent with creators about how it works, fair in how it distributes opportunities, and genuinely invested in the success of the people who create on it.

Today, Vitox serves creators and viewers across the globe, with a growing library of videos spanning gaming, music, education, tech, comedy, news, and much more. We are constantly improving, constantly listening to our community, and constantly working to make Vitox the best place to create and watch video content on the internet.

Our Values

Creator First. Every decision we make at Vitox starts with the question: "How does this help creators?" We believe that when creators succeed, the platform succeeds.

Transparency. We are open about how our algorithms work, how revenue is distributed, and how we make decisions that impact the community. No hidden tricks, no confusing policies.

Authenticity. We celebrate genuine content and real creativity. Vitox is not a place for spam, misinformation, or low-quality content farms.

Community. The relationship between creators and their audience is sacred. We build tools that strengthen that bond, not exploit it.

Innovation. We are always working on new features, better tools, and smarter systems to help creators, viewers, and advertisers get more value from Vitox.

Content Policy

At Vitox, we believe in freedom of expression within a framework of responsibility. Our platform is open to all genres of content, but we maintain strict guidelines to ensure a safe, respectful, and legal environment for everyone.

We do not allow content that is: sexually explicit, hateful or discriminatory, violent or promoting harm, spam or auto-generated, or in violation of copyright law. All creators agree to our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines when signing up.

Content moderation is handled by a combination of automated systems and human review. If you believe content violates our policies, you can report it directly on the platform.
