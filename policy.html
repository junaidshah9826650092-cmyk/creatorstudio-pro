Policy & Safety

Keeping Vitox safe for everyone

Our Commitment: Vitox is dedicated to providing a safe, respectful, and creative environment for all users. We enforce strict policies to protect our community from harm, harassment, and inappropriate content.

Safety First

We do not tolerate behavior that threatens, abuses, or endangers others. The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Hate Speech: Content that promotes violence or incites hatred against individuals or groups based on race, ethnic origin, religion, disability, age, nationality, veteran status, sexual orientation, gender, or gender identity.
  • Harassment & Cyberbullying: Content that targets individuals with malicious intent, persistent abuse, or threatening behavior.
  • Exploitation: Any content that exploits minors or promotes illegal acts.

Content Restrictions

To ensure Vitox remains a platform for general audiences, certain types of content are restricted or prohibited:

  • Explicit Content: Pornography and sexually explicit content are not allowed.
  • Violent or Graphic Content: Content that is overly gruesome or intended to shock or disgust viewers.
  • Dangerous Activities: Videos promoting activities that have a serious risk of physical harm or death.

Account Security

Protecting your account is critical. We recommend using strong passwords through your Google account and enabling two-factor authentication. Vitox staff will never ask for your password or sensitive personal information.

Reporting Violations
